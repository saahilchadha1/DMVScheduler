Information

Hi! I'm Saahil. I'm a college student from Minnesota, and I'm a pretty bad driver (a.k.a I took the Road Test 5 times). This also means that I was extremely frustrated by the online Road Test Scheduler here. I remember very unfondly the hours I spent refreshing the page over and over again hoping that someway in hell a spot would open up and I could take my Road Test. I also remember thinking there has to be a better way. Well, now there is!
